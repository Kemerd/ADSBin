ADSBin adapts (does not copy verbatim) the following antirez dump1090 portions,
all of which are covered by this BSD 3-Clause license and which carry a preserved
copyright/attribution notice in their source headers:
  - components/demod1090/demod1090.c  (magnitude-LUT envelope, 8 us preamble
    correlation, PPM half-bit Manchester slicing — generalised to 2.4 Msps)
  - components/modes_decode/modes_decode.c  (table-driven 24-bit Mode-S CRC,
    poly 0xFFF409; DF17/18 parity -> ICAO recovery; Gillham Mode-C altitude fold)
  - components/modes_decode/cpr.c  (CPR global/local decode + NL longitude-zone
    decoder structure; NL boundary numbers independently keyed from the spec)
